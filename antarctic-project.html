Antarctic

Antarctic is a horror game inspired by Lovecraft's work and The Thing movie.

Description

This is a WIP project that came out of the 2 month long internship at Autentikum studio. I was the only programmer on a team of around 10 of my classmates, including concept artists, 3D artists, game designers and level designers. The goal was to create a vertical slice by ourselves with weekly meetings and feedback from the mentors at the studio.

1. Programming Responsibilities
 

Programming Responsibilities and Successes

  • Designed and kept a clean extendable code architecture

Team Oriented Responsibilities and Successes
