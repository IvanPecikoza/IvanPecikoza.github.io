Wardens of Ether

Wardens of Ether is a dark fantasy Deck Building Roguelike.

Features

  • Use cards as attacks and abilities similar to Slay the Spire
  • Gain rewards after every fight and encounter
  • Rest at the camp site and use your camp actions to new cards and artifacts
  • Use corruption to your advantage and change card effects, but be careful not to die to it
  • Experiment with different effect interactions to build a powerful deck and defeat the boss
